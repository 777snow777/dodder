Welcome to Dodder

this is a server inspired by Cuscuta.. otherwise known as dodder
due to its weak photosynthetic capacity, dodder must find a host within the first few days after germinating in soil or it will die
after finding a host, dodder twists around it and inserts a haustoria into the stem of the host. it begins to draw water and nutrients through the haustoria from the host and its anchoring root dies. it sends more haustorias into its host.
dodder exchanges millions of mRNA molecules with its host, creating a dialogue with the other plant. it uses mRNA to encode messages that elicit a specific response from its host.
dodder can attach to multiple plants at once and send signals between these plants. this is called a dodder web.
when the health of one of the host plants is threatened, it can send messages through the dodder web to nearby plants to prepare a defense
